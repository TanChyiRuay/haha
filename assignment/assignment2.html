Recipe for grilled cheese sandwiches with bacon and tomato

list of ingredients:

  • Square bread, two slices

  • Eggs, one

  • Cheese, one slice

  • Butter, a little

  • Tomato slices, to taste

  • Bacon, one slice

Instructions:

First, fry the bacon. According to the taste of the individual, set aside the fried bacon and wait for the oil to drain. Then spread the bread with butter on one side and egg wash on the other side. Then turn on a low heat, put the buttered side down, put it in a hot pan, fry until browned, and then turn it over. Wait until the egg liquid has a fragrant egg flavor, then turn it over. Then quickly put the cut cheese on the egg mixture and fry on a low fire for about two minutes. Turn off the heat when the cheese has melted. Then put bacon and tomato slices on two slices of bread.
